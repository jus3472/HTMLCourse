Here is my Breakfast Recipe

Let me tell you how I like to my breakfast bacon egg and cheese sandwich

  1. Take two slices of multigrain bread and toast it on the setting: 2.
  2. Take out your pan and set the heat to medium for 2 minutes.
  3. Place 3 pieces of bacon directly on the pan without oil.
  4. After cooking each side for 2 minutes, place the bacon on your toast.
  5. Put half a spoon of butter on the pan and crack two eggs.
  6. Flip the eggs after 2 minutes and gently polk the yolk. This creates the perfect mixture of wet to dry yolk.
  7. After cooking for another minute, place the eggs on top of the bacon.
  8. Turn the heat to medium-low and place one slice of provolone on it.
  9. Move the provlone around and take it out of the pan depending on how melty you like your cheese.
  10. Once your cheese is on your sandwich, place the top slice of toast on top, and congratulations, you just made my famous bacon egg and cheese sandwich!

Here is my Chicken Recipe

Let me tell you how I cook my garlic roasted chicken

  1. Season the chicken with salt, black pepper, and garlic.
  2. Take out your pan and put the heat to medium.
  3. Wait 2 minutes, put a spoon of oil, then wait another minute.
  4. Place your chicken in the pan. Make sure to place it away from you.
  5. After 5 minutes, flip the chicken with your spatula.
  6. Add 3 garlic cloves and half a spoon of butter.
  7. Lower the temperature to medium-low and baste the chicken with butter.
  8. Cook for another 4 minutes.
  9. When evenly cooked, place your chicken on a plate and let it rest for 4 minutes before digging in!

Here is my Steak Recipe

Let me tell you how I cook my world famous steak!

  1. Season your steak generously with Montreal Steak Seasoning.
  2. Take out your pan and set the heat to medium-high for 3 minutes.
  3. Put one spoon of oil in the pan and wait another minute.
  4. Lay your steak away from you onto the pan so that the oil doesn't spash on you.
  5. Lower the heat to medium and cook the steak for 5 minutes. Feel free the pat down parts of the steak that aren't touching the pan fully.
  6. Flip after 5 minutes and add one spoon of butter.
  7. Lower the heat to medium-low and add thyme, rosemary, and 4 garlic cloves while basting the steak.
  8. Increase the heat back to medium and cook for another 4 minutes.
  9. Take the steak out of the pan and place it on your plate to rest for 4 minutes before cutting and eating!

Here is my Pasta Recipe

Let me tell you how I like to make pasta

  1. Take out your spaghetti and boil it in a large pot for 10 minutes for al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, I like to cook some chicken using my grilled chicken recipe. After the chicken is done, I slice it in long bite size pieces and set it aside.
  3. Once the pasta is done cooking, set it aside in a strainer.
  4. In the same pan used to cook the chicken, turn the heat back to medium and add a spoon of oil.
  5. After waiting for another minute, add some minced garlic and oregano into the pan.
  6. After 30 seconds, add a couple of large spoons of Marinara sauce and stir.
  7. Turn the heat down to medium-low and cover the pan with a lid to simmer the sauce for 4 minutes.
  8. After 4 minutes, add your spaghetti and chicken into the pan and stir.
  9. After 3 minutes, transfer your pasta onto a plate and enjoy!
